About Me

Hello. my name is Meesum, I was born in Karachi Pakistan and moved to America in 2009 when I was 12. I moved to OC California and have been living here since. I recently just completed my bachelors at UC San Diego in Aerospace Engineering.

While at UCSD I was a part of an engineering club called Yonder Dynamics. There I worked with other students to make a mars rover for the university rover challenge. My favorite part was applying engineering principles and being able to create tangible object after learning about things in school. But I quickly left that club because I felt I would benefit more by focusing on my studies and classwork

Therefore, after I graduated, I found myself working as a mechanical engineer for a company called ASML. There I worked as a mechanical install engineer and installed Extreme Ultraviolet lithography machines. These machines were highly complex with over 1000 components and 1500 procedures. My fav part was working with innovative technology and traveling to different places to perform installs. However, the role was very demanding in terms of travel and was not the best for a good work-life balance.

Due to my Interest in coding previous introduction to coding I decided to pursue a career as a software engineer which led me to develop my skills at General Assembly.
